Pickles

The dog that found the World Cup trophy

Pickles (born 1962 or 1963; died 1967) was known for his role in finding the stolen Jules Rimet Trophy in March 1966, four months before the 1966 FIFA World Cup was scheduled to kick off in England.

  • The World Cup trophy was stolen on the afternoon of Sunday 20 March 1966.
